PERSCOM provides a vast array of different options to integrate your personnel data with third-party services. These services include the PERSCOM API, Command Line Interface (CLI), OAuth 2.0, OpenID Connect (OIDC), Webhooks, Widgets and more. Visit the individual integrations to view more information and how-to's on how to integrate with these services.
